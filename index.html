About Me

Hello there! I'm Geetesh, a developer and aspiring UX designer based in Nashik, India.

Having spent a large chunk of my life in engineering college, I've finally found my passion at the crossroads of design and technology. I am an occasional purveyor of wisdom and a tireless seeker of proficiency. Currently, I am pursuing IT engineering from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, Pune, India.
